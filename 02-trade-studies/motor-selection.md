## Selection Rationale

**Why hub motor over mid-drive?**
- Simpler drivetrain — no chain, sprocket, or reduction gearbox to design/source
- Lower maintenance
- Direct fit into existing wheel with 19" rim
- Tradeoff: more unsprung mass, less torque multiplication
