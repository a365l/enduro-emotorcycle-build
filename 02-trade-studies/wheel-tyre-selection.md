# Trade Study: Wheel & Tyre Selection

## Decision

Phase 2: Fitted **19" × 80/100 spoke rim** to rear to match QS205 motor axle and provide appropriate tyre profile for enduro use.

---

## Requirements

- Must accept QS205 hub motor axle (check axle width and dropout spacing)
- Appropriate tyre profile for trail / enduro use
- Strong enough spoke rim for off-road loading

## Effect on Performance

- Larger wheel diameter → higher top speed for same motor RPM (see `top-speed-prediction.md`)
- 80/100 profile provides adequate ground clearance and traction for trail use
- Spoke rim allows tyre repair / re-lacing vs cast wheel
